Home page

Welcome to Coup! In the not too distant future, the government is run for profit by a new "royal class" of multinational CEOs. Their greed and absolute control of the economy has reduced all but a privileged few to lives of poverty and desperation. Out of the oppressed masses rose The Resistance, an underground organization focused on overthrowing these powerful rulers. The valiant efforts of The Resistance have created discord, intrigue, and weakness in the political courts of the noveau royal, bringing the government to brink of collapse. But for you, a powerful government official, this is your opportunity to manipulate, bribe and bluff your way into absolute power. To be successful, you must destroy the influence of your rivals and drive them into exile. In these turbulent times there is only room for one to survive. (Taken from the instructions and the game itself) As the newest member of the Resistance one may chose to either participate in our training program or simply show us initialtive and dive right in!

Insturctions: Set Up: Shuffle all cards, then deal 2 to each player. Place the remaining cards on the table face down in a deck. This is the court deck Each player gets 2 coins at the begining. Remaining coins are placed in the center of the table. This is the treasury. Player to the right of the dealer, or who won last goes first. Gameplay: Play goes in a clockwise motion. Actions: Each player gets exactly one action per turn. On turn the player may take any action. Some actions cost coins, if a player does not have the proper amount of coins they may not take that action. Some actions are immediate and can be played at anytime. If a player is challenged on a character action. Challenge must be resolved first. If player wins challenge then action proceeds. If opponent wins the challenge the action is aborted. Challenges: On any character action a player may challenge the person claiming that action. If challenger wins the challenge then opponent loses a card. The winner then replaces the challenged card with a new one from the court deck. The challenged card goes into the court deck If challenger loses the challenge then they must lose a card. Defeat: DEFEATED CARDS ARE PLACED FACE UP ON THE TABLE!!! A Player is defeated when both cards are face up on the table. They may no longer participate. There are no more than 3 of each card in the game. Win Conditions A player may win when all other opponents are defeated. Action List: Generic Actions: Income: Player takes on coin from the treasury Foreign aid: Player takes 2 coins from the treasury Coup: MUST BE PERFORMED IF PLAYER HAS MORE THAN 10 COINS. Cannot be blocked. Pay 7 coins to defeat an opponents card. Character Actions: Ambassador: Look at the top 2 cards of the court deck. You may exchange your cards with them. Place them back on top of The court deck in a random order. Can block stealing Assassin: Pay 3 coins, defeat another card. Captain: Steals 2 coins from another player; Contessa: Blocks Assassination. Duke: Take 3 from the treasury, blocks Foreign aid
